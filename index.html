Date Apps

A collection of single-purpose date-calculation utility apps.

App Description
Date Calculator Apply a day/week/month/year offset to a date.
Pregnancy Due Date Calculator Estimate a due date from LMP (Naegele's Rule).
Dose Counter Figure out how many pills remain to prescribe for a course of medication.
Pediatric Dosing Weight-based dosing for common pediatric medications (currently acetaminophen).
